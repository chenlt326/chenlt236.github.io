Glad to have the opportunity to participate in the 9th National Urban Meteorological Forum （Chinese: 第九届全国城市气象学术论坛） held by Chinese Meteorological Society in Xiong An, where I presented my research.

This was my first in-person conference in mainland, and it was incredibly rewarding to meet and exchange ideas with many domestic peers. I am honored to have received the Best Paper Award at the forum.
