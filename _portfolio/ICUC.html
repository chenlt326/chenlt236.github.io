I had the opportunity to attend the 11th International Conference on Urban Climate (ICUC) in Sydney and present my research. It was an incredibly exciting and fulfilling experience.

Meeting my academic idols during the conference was truly inspiring and motivating. Interacting with them and learning about their research perspectives and experiences was invaluable.

Moreover, reconnecting with old friends from ResearchGate in person at the ICUC conference was a delight. Despite our previous virtual interactions, meeting face-to-face fostered a strong sense of community and friendship. We shared our research progress and offered support and encouragement to each other.

I am grateful to the ICUC conference for providing me with this valuable opportunity, and I want to express my appreciation to everyone who has supported and encouraged me. I will continue striving for academic excellence and contributing to the advancement of scientific research.
